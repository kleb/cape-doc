Conversion tools for pyCart¶

Perform conversions such as (alpha total, phi) to (alpha, beta).

pyCart.convert.AlphaBeta2AlphaTPhi(alpha, beta)¶

Convert total angle of attack and total roll angle to angle of attack and sideslip angle.

Call:
>>> alpha_t, phi = pyCart.AlphaBeta2AlphaTPhi(alpha, beta)
Inputs:
alpha: float or numpy.array

Angle of attack

beta: float or numpy.array

Sideslip angle
Outputs:
alpha_t: float or numpy.array

Total angle of attack

phi: float or numpy.array

Total roll angle
Versions:
  • 2014-06-02 @ddalle: First version
  • 2014-11-05 @ddalle: Transposed alpha and beta in w formula
